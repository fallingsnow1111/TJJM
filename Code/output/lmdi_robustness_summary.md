# LMDI Robustness and Extensions

## 1. Identification boundary
本文采用恒等式分解而非因果识别，因此结论应解释为贡献分解，不应直接表述为结构性因果效应。GDP、能源强度与产业结构之间存在耦合关系，LMDI 的目标是将这种耦合拆解为可解释贡献，而不是估计独立结构参数。

## 2. Residual diagnostics
- Full sample residual mean: 1.890e-14
- Full sample residual median: 4.604e-15
- Full sample residual p90: 3.237e-14
- Full sample residual max: 3.602e-12
- Interpretation: residuals stay near zero, so the five-factor decomposition is internally consistent.

## 3. Variable-definition robustness
### 3.1 Five-factor vs four-factor specification
- Five-factor totals: P=1081.892, A=20912.054, S=-1459.902, B=-10955.640, C=-1004.766, net=8573.637
- Four-factor totals: P=1081.892, A=20912.054, B=-12415.543, C=-1004.766
- The structural term S is not redundant: once industry structure is separated, the energy-intensity term B shifts from -10955.640 to -12415.543, showing that structural adjustment had been embedded inside the composite intensity term in the four-factor specification.
- This comparison supports using the five-factor version as the preferred specification in the paper body, with the four-factor version reported as a robustness benchmark.

## 4. Sample-window robustness
### 4.1 Baseline vs late-sample re-estimation
- Baseline sample (1990-2022) residual mean: 1.890e-14
- Late sample (2000-2022) residual mean: 2.052e-14
- Sign patterns are preserved in both samples: GDP per capita remains the dominant positive driver, while energy intensity remains the main negative driver.
- Practical reading: the main narrative is not generated by early-period interpolation alone; it survives when the sample is restricted to the later period.

## 5. Data-processing robustness
### 5.1 Observed-energy subset
- Observed-energy subset residual mean: 1.848e-14
- Observed-energy subset pair count: 960
- This check is stricter because it drops rows marked as national-proxy energy. The fact that residuals remain tiny indicates that the decomposition identity itself is not sensitive to the energy reconstruction step.

## 9. Paper-facing conclusion
1. The five-factor specification is preferable because it isolates industry structure from energy intensity.
2. Residual diagnostics show near-zero discrepancy, so the decomposition is numerically stable.
3. The main narrative survives both later-sample and observed-energy restrictions, reducing the risk that the result is an artifact of one data-processing choice.
